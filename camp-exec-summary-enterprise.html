Why Act Now

Force 01 — Productivity

17.3 hours lost per developer, per week

The average developer spends 17.3 hours each week on maintenance and technical debt rather than building. Innovation becomes nearly impossible and burnout accelerates. — McKinsey

Force 02 — Talent

Legacy skills are disappearing

Graduates are trained on modern, cloud-native stacks. Expertise in legacy systems grows scarcer every year, making those positions harder to fill and the systems harder to change safely.

Force 03 — Risk

70% cite technical debt as their innovation blocker

Unpatched legacy systems are security vulnerabilities. Compliance requirements evolve while systems remain static. 78% of financial services organisations say debt blocks new feature delivery. — Protiviti
