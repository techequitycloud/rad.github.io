# Buy Credits

The **Buy Credits** tab allows users to purchase additional credits or subscribe to a monthly plan. Purchasing a monthly plan unlocks additional platform features available exclusively to partners. Purchasing sufficient credits ensures uninterrupted access to deploying and managing modules.

## Features

*   **Subscription Plans:** View available monthly subscription tiers configured by the administrator.
*   **One-Time Purchases:** (If configured) Options to buy a fixed amount of credits instantly.
*   **Secure Checkout:** Integrated with Stripe for secure payment processing.

## How to Purchase

1.  Navigate to the **Credits** page and click on the **Buy Credits** tab.
2.  Review the available **Subscription Tiers**. Each card displays:
    *   Tier Name and Description
    *   Monthly Price
    *   Included Credits
    *   Key Features
3.  Click the **Subscribe** or **Buy Now** button on your chosen plan.
4.  You will be redirected to a secure Stripe checkout page.
5.  Complete the payment information.
6.  Upon success, you will be redirected back to the application, and your credit balance will be updated immediately.

## Troubleshooting

*   **Credits not appearing?** It may take a few moments for the transaction to sync. Try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, check the **Credit Transactions** tab for a record of the purchase or contact support.
