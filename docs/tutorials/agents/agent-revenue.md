# Tracking Your Agent Revenue

This tutorial is for RAD Platform agents. It explains how to use the **Credits** page to monitor the revenue generated from your modules and the users you manage.

## What You'll Learn

- How to navigate to the agent revenue dashboards.
- How to interpret the **User Revenue** report.
- How to track earnings from your modules in the **Module Revenue** report.

### Prerequisites

- A RAD Platform account with **Agent** status.

### Estimated Time

- **10 minutes**

---

## Step 1: Navigate to the Credits Page

All of your revenue and commission information is available on the **Credits** page.

1.  From the main navigation menu, click on **"Credits"**.
    ![Credits Menu](/img/site/2.5-agent-credits-user_revenue.png)

## Step 2: Review User Revenue

The **User Revenue** tab shows the commissions you have earned from the credit purchases of users assigned to you.

1.  Click on the **"User Revenue"** tab.
2.  You will see a dashboard with:
    -   A summary of your total earnings.
    -   A detailed breakdown of revenue per user.
    -   The commission rate for each transaction.
    ![User Revenue Dashboard](/img/site/2.5-agent-credits-user_revenue.png)

## Step 3: Track Module Revenue

If you are also a partner who publishes modules, the **Module Revenue** tab shows the earnings generated from your modules being deployed by other users.

1.  Click on the **"Module Revenue"** tab.
2.  This dashboard displays:
    -   Total revenue generated by each of your modules.
    -   The number of times each module has been deployed.
    ![Module Revenue Dashboard](/img/site/2.6-agent-credits-module_revenue.png)

## Verification

You have successfully learned how to track your revenue as a RAD Platform agent. You can now monitor your earnings from both user commissions and module deployments.

-   If you are a partner, consider creating new modules to increase your potential revenue.
