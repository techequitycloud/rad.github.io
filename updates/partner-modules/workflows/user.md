# User Workflows

This document outlines the daily workflows for **Users** (Members). Whether you are an individual or a small business, these guides help you manage your account and perform transactions.

## Dashboard Overview

Upon logging in, the **Dashboard** gives you a quick summary of your account:
*   **Account Balance**: Your current funds.
*   **Quick Actions**: Shortcuts to "Pay User", "Directory", etc.
*   **Recent Activity**: Your latest transactions and notifications.

## Making Payments

### Paying Another User
1.  Click **Pay User** or go to **Banking** > **Payment to User**.
2.  **Search** for the recipient by name or login (or select from your contacts).
3.  Enter the **Amount**.
4.  Add a **Description** (e.g., "Lunch share", "Invoice #123").
5.  Click **Next** to review details.
6.  Click **Confirm** to complete the transfer.

### Paying a Business (Scan QR)
1.  If using the mobile app, tap the **QR Code** icon.
2.  Scan the merchant's QR code.
3.  Verify the merchant name and amount.
4.  Confirm the payment.

## Account History

### Viewing Transactions
1.  Go to **Banking** > **Account History** (or simply click on your balance).
2.  You will see a list of all incoming and outgoing payments.
3.  Click on any transaction to see full details or print a receipt.
4.  Use the **Filter** options to search by date range or keyword.

## Directory & Contacts

### Finding Users
1.  Go to **Marketplace** or **Directory**.
2.  Use the search bar to find businesses or other members.
3.  Filter by category (e.g., "Food", "Services").
4.  View their profile to see their products, location, or to send a message.

### Managing Contacts
1.  When viewing a user's profile, click **Add to Contacts**.
2.  This saves them to your contact list for faster payments in the future.
3.  Go to **Personal** > **Contacts** to manage your list.

## Profile & Settings

### Updating Your Profile
1.  Click on your name or avatar > **Profile**.
2.  Click **Edit** to update your phone number, address, or bio.
3.  Upload a profile picture to help others recognize you.

### Changing Password
1.  Go to **Personal** > **Password**.
2.  Enter your old password and your new password.
3.  Save changes.

### Notifications
1.  Go to **Settings** > **Notifications**.
2.  Choose how you want to be notified (Email, SMS, App Push) for different events like receiving payments or new messages.
