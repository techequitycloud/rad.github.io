# Partner Workflows

This document outlines the workflows for **Partners**, **Merchants**, and **Business Members**. These users utilize the platform to accept payments, manage business transactions, and promote their services.

## Accepting Payments

### Receive Payment (QR Code)
1.  Log in to the mobile app or web interface.
2.  Navigate to **Receive Payment**.
3.  Enter the amount (optional) and description.
4.  Generate the **QR Code**.
5.  Show the code to the customer to scan and pay.

### Payment Requests
1.  Navigate to **Banking** > **Payment Request**.
2.  Select the customer (User).
3.  Enter the amount and description/invoice number.
4.  Click **Send**.
5.  The customer receives a notification to confirm the payment.

### Point of Sale (POS)
If your account is enabled for POS:
1.  Open the **POS** interface.
2.  Enter the sale amount.
3.  Identify the customer (by QR code scan, card, or login name).
4.  Process the transaction.

## Managing Sales

### Transaction History
1.  Go to **Account** > **History**.
2.  View all incoming payments.
3.  Filter by date or user to find specific sales.
4.  Click on a transaction to view details (timestamp, description, fees).

### Refunds (Chargebacks)
1.  Locate the transaction in your history.
2.  Click the transaction to open details.
3.  Click **Chargeback** or **Refund**.
4.  Confirm the action. *Note: You may have a time limit for performing self-service refunds.*

## Business Profile

### Directory Listing
Partners are often listed in the business directory.
1.  Go to **Profile** > **Edit Profile**.
2.  Ensure your **Business Name**, **Category**, and **Description** are up to date.
3.  Add **Location** (Address/Map) so customers can find you.
4.  Upload a **Logo** or banner image.

## Managing Operators
Partners can create "Operators" (staff members) who can access the account with limited permissions (e.g., Cashiers).

1.  Navigate to **Settings** > **Operators** (or **Users**).
2.  Click **New Operator**.
3.  Create a login for your staff member.
4.  Assign permissions (e.g., "Can accept payments" but "Cannot view history").
