# Project Invoices (User View)

The **Project Invoices** tab allows users to view monthly summaries of their credit usage and any associated costs.

## Features

*   **Monthly View:** Select a specific month to see that period's invoice.
*   **Usage Summary:** A summary of total credits consumed during the month.
*   **Download:** Option to download a PDF or CSV version of the invoice (if enabled).

## How to Use

1.  Navigate to the **Credits** page and click on the **Project Invoices** tab.
2.  Select the **Month** and **Year** you wish to view.
3.  The report will load, showing your usage for that billing cycle.
