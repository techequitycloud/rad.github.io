# Tutorial: Agent Programme

## Overview

The Agent Programme lets you earn revenue by referring new users to the platform. When someone signs up using your referral code and makes paid deployments, you earn a commission on their activity. This tutorial explains how to find your referral code, track the users you have referred, and understand how your earnings are calculated.

**Audience:** Users with the **Agent** role  
**Estimated time:** 10 minutes

By the end of this tutorial you will have:
- Located and shared your unique referral link
- Understood how referred users are tracked in the system
- Reviewed the Revenue dashboard to monitor your earnings

---

## Step 1: Get Your Referral Code

1. Click **Help** in the top navigation bar.
2. Locate the **Invite User** card (this section is only visible when the referral programme is enabled by your platform administrator).
3. You will see two sharing options:
   - **Referral Link** — Copy the full URL and share it via email, social media, or any messaging platform.
   - **QR Code** — Display or print the QR code for in-person sharing.
4. When a new user signs up using your link or code, they are automatically linked to your account.

> **Tip:** The referral link includes your unique agent identifier. Sharing it on multiple channels does not create duplicate accounts — any sign-up that uses your link is attributed to you regardless of where the link was shared from.

---

## Step 2: Track Your Referred Users

Once users start signing up through your referral link, you can monitor their activity.

1. Click **Revenue** in the navigation bar.
2. You will be on the **My Referral Revenue** tab by default.
3. Use the **user dropdown** to select a specific referred user and view their individual activity, or leave it on the summary view to see aggregated data across all your referrals.
4. The dashboard shows:

   | Metric | Description |
   | :--- | :--- |
   | **Referred Users** | Total number of users who signed up using your referral link |
   | **Active Users** | Referred users who have made at least one paid deployment |
   | **True Revenue** | The total platform revenue generated by your referred users' deployments |
   | **Your Commission** | Your share of that revenue, calculated at the commission rate set by the platform |

> **Note:** Commission is calculated only on **paid** deployments (those that consume purchased credits). Deployments funded entirely by sign-up bonuses or free monthly credits do not contribute to your revenue figure.

---

## Step 3: Understand Payouts

Earnings accumulate in your agent account and are paid out on a regular schedule.

- **Payout frequency:** Payouts are processed monthly, typically on the first business day of the following month.
- **Minimum threshold:** A minimum earning balance may be required before a payout is initiated — contact your platform administrator for the specific threshold.
- **Payout method:** Your payout method (bank transfer, payment platform, or other) is configured by the platform administrator. Contact the support team if you need to update your payout details or if you have a query about a specific payment.

> **Note:** Payout schedules and commission rates are set at the platform level by an Administrator. If you have questions about your specific rate or payment timeline, raise a support request via the **Help > Support** tab.
