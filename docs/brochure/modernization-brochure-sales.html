Where ISV Partners Lose Deals
Friction 01 — Proof of Concept Speed

POC environments take 3–5 days to build — per application

A meaningful modernization POC touches VPC, IAM, CI/CD, databases, observability, security controls and a deployed application simultaneously. Getting all of that production-grade before a client can evaluate it burns days of senior consultant time — time you are paying for before a deal is signed. Prospects lose patience. Competitors move faster.

Friction 02 — Repeatability

Every POC starts from a blank page

Without a standardised demo and POC platform, each consultant builds environments differently. Knowledge is trapped in individuals. Best practices are not enforced. Each new deal requires the same expensive ramp-up — and the quality of your proof varies with the experience of whoever happens to be staffed that week.

Friction 03 — Business Case Clarity

Buyers need numbers — and most partners can't provide them

Decision-makers approve cloud modernization investments on ROI, not architecture diagrams. Without a credible, client-specific business case — quantifying time saved, cost reduced, and risk mitigated — deals stall in procurement. Partners who arrive with proof arrive with power.
