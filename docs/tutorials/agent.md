## 1. Introduction

This tutorial is for Agents. You will learn how to find your referral link and track the revenue generated by the users you invite.

## 2. Step 1: Get Your Referral Link

1.  Navigate to the **Help** page.
2.  Click the **Support** tab.
3.  Locate the **Invite Friends** card.
4.  Copy your unique referral link (e.g., `https://techequity.cloud?ref=YOUR_CODE`) or download the QR code.

## 3. Step 2: Track Your Referrals

1.  Click **Revenue** in the main navigation bar.
2.  Select the **User Revenue** tab.
3.  This table lists all users who signed up using your link.
4.  Check the **True Revenue** column to see how much revenue they have generated for the platform.

## 4. Step 3: Analyze Module Performance

1.  Switch to the **Module Revenue** tab.
2.  See which modules your referred users are deploying most frequently.
3.  Use this data to tailor your marketing or recommendations.
