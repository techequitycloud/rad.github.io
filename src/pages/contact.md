# Contact

## Documentation issues

Found an error, broken link, or outdated instruction in these docs?
[Open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/techequitycloud/rad.github.io/issues)
— this is the fastest way to get documentation problems fixed.

## Platform support

For questions about deployments, credits, or your account, use the in-app
support options in the [RAD Console](https://radmodules.dev).

## Partnerships and training programs

Interested in delivering Google Cloud certification training with RAD for your
organization or cohort? Reach out via
[LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/company/techequitycloud) or open a
[GitHub discussion](https://github.com/techequitycloud).
