# Agent Workflows

This document outlines the workflows for **Agents** (also known as **Brokers**) in Cyclos. Agents act as intermediaries who manage a set of members, facilitating their registration, account management, and transactions.

## Managing Members

### Registering a New Member
Agents can register new members into their broker group.

1.  Log in to your Agent account.
2.  Navigate to **My Members** or **Brokerage**.
3.  Click **Register New Member**.
4.  Select the appropriate **Group** (e.g., "Member").
5.  Fill in the new member's details (Name, Email, etc.).
6.  Click **Submit**. The new member is now assigned to you.

### Viewing Member Overview
1.  Go to **My Members**.
2.  You will see a list of all members assigned to you.
3.  Use the search bar to find a specific member.
4.  Click on a member to view their profile, account balance, and recent activity.

## Performing Actions for Members

### Making a Payment on Behalf of a Member
As an agent, you may have permission to perform payments for your members.

1.  Navigate to **My Members** and select a member.
2.  On their profile, click **Payment** or **Account**.
3.  Select **Payment to System** or **Payment to User**.
4.  Enter the amount and description.
5.  Confirm the transaction.

### Managing Member Advertisements
1.  Select a member from your list.
2.  Navigate to their **Advertisements** section.
3.  You can create new ads or manage existing ones for the member.

## Account & Commission

### Viewing Commissions
Agents often earn commissions on member transaction fees or membership fees.

1.  Go to **Account** > **History**.
2.  Filter transactions by type to see "Commission" or "Brokerage Fee".
3.  Review your earnings over a specific period.

### Monthly Statements
1.  Navigate to **Reports** > **Account Statement**.
2.  Select the period.
3.  Download the statement (PDF/CSV) to review your brokerage activities and earnings.
