## 1. Introduction
This tutorial walks through the Return on Investment (ROI) calculator on the RAD platform. The ROI calculator helps you quantify the time and cost savings achieved by automating infrastructure deployments with RAD compared to manual provisioning workflows.

## 2. Step 1: Access the ROI Calculator
1. Click **ROI** in the top navigation bar.
2. The ROI Calculator page will load with interactive input fields and a summary dashboard.

## 3. Step 2: Configure Your Inputs
Fill in the fields to match your organisation's deployment profile:

1. **Number of Deployments per Month:** Enter the average number of infrastructure deployments your team performs each month.
2. **Average Manual Deployment Time (hours):** Enter the estimated hours it takes to manually provision and configure an equivalent deployment without RAD.
3. **Hourly Engineering Cost:** Enter the fully-loaded hourly cost of an engineer performing the manual work (salary, benefits, overhead).
4. **RAD Deployment Time (hours):** Enter the average time RAD takes to complete the same deployment (typically a fraction of the manual time).

## 4. Step 3: Review the Results
After entering your inputs, the calculator automatically computes:

- **Time Saved per Month:** Total engineer hours recovered by using RAD.
- **Cost Saved per Month:** Monthly cost savings based on your hourly engineering rate.
- **Annual Cost Savings:** Projected yearly savings extrapolated from monthly figures.
- **Payback Period:** How quickly the platform investment pays for itself based on savings.

## 5. Step 4: Export or Share
- Use your browser's print or PDF export function to save the results for stakeholder reporting.
- Share the figures with your finance or leadership team to support platform adoption decisions.
