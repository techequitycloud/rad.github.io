The Skills Deficit

The demand for cloud computing expertise is outpacing supply globally. 34% of technology leaders specifically cite cloud computing as the top skill set they are failing to fill. For organisations that are adopting Google Cloud, the gap between acquiring licences and realising business value can span years — costing considerably more than the cloud spend itself.

This gap is not just a technical problem. It is an organisational design problem, a process maturity problem, and a cultural problem. Research by Google (DORA 2024) shows that only 19% of engineering teams currently operate at elite performance level, while 25% remain in the lowest performance band — deploying infrequently, recovering slowly from failures, and generating high rates of change-driven incidents.
