Executive Profile

Shiyghan Navti is one of the most experienced Google Cloud transformation practitioners operating globally today. Since 2018, he has held a trusted position within Google Cloud's partner and client enablement ecosystem, consistently delivering more than 50 engagements per year across Europe, the Middle East and Africa, North America, Asia Pacific, and Latin America. Over the course of that relationship, he has personally trained more than 10,000 professionals and completed more than 500 unique client engagements on the Google Cloud platform — achieving a customer satisfaction score of 4.8 out of 5.0 across Google's own post-engagement surveys. He is widely regarded within the Google Cloud community as one of the most technically and academically qualified members of the extended trainer and partner network.

In parallel to his Google Cloud delivery work, Shiyghan has led the development of RAD (Rapid Application Deployment), a proprietary Internal Developer Platform built on Google Cloud that operationalises cloud best practices as deployable, production-ready infrastructure. RAD serves as the hands-on delivery vehicle for a structured Cloud Transformation Accelerator Program — a six-program, 24-session curriculum grounded in Google's DORA research, the Google Cloud Adoption Framework, Site Reliability Engineering, DevSecOps, Platform Engineering, FinOps, Team Topologies, the NIST Cybersecurity Framework, and Generative AI — designed to take organisations from baseline maturity assessment through to a continuously improving, AI-ready cloud operating model.

Shiyghan's depth of expertise is underpinned by an unusually broad career spanning rigorous academic research, enterprise technology leadership, and entrepreneurship. His enterprise career includes senior leadership roles at IBM, where he led cloud technical sales across Europe and drove the organisation's Africa geo-expansion strategy, and at Vodafone Group, where he built and led the Cloud Advisory Group responsible for developing hybrid cloud services in partnership with Google, AWS, and Microsoft. He has delivered consulting and advisory services to numerous Fortune 100 organisations, the World Bank, and the Commonwealth Telecommunications Organization. For a digital transformation partnership, he brings a rare combination of academic rigour, technical depth, Google ecosystem credentials, and over two decades of enterprise leadership across multiple industries and geographies.
