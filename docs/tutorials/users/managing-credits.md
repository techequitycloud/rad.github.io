# Managing Your Credits

This tutorial explains how to manage your billing and credits on the RAD Platform. You will learn how to check your credit balance, purchase additional credits, and track your spending.

## What You'll Learn

- How to navigate the **Credits** page.
- How to make one-time credit purchases.
- How to request credits from an administrator.
- How to monitor your credit transactions and project costs.

### Prerequisites

- A RAD Platform account.
- You have completed the [Your First Deployment](./first-deployment.md) tutorial.

### Estimated Time

- **10 minutes**

---

## Step 1: Navigate to the Credits Page

All billing-related activities are managed from the **Credits** page.

1.  From the main navigation menu on the left, click on **"Credits"**.

    ![Main navigation menu with "Credits" highlighted](/img/site/2-user-credits-menu.png)

2.  The **Credits** page has several tabs: **Buy Credits**, **Credit Transactions**, **Project Costs**, and **Monthly Invoices**.

    ![Credits page with all the tabs visible](/img/site/2.1-user-credits-buy_credits.png)

## Step 2: Purchase Credits

If you need more credits, you can make a one-time purchase.

1.  Click on the **"Buy Credits"** tab.
2.  Enter the number of credits you wish to purchase.
3.  Click the **"Buy Credits"** button and you will be redirected to Stripe to complete the purchase.

    ![Stripe payment page for one-time purchase](/img/site/2.1.2-user-credits-buy_credits-stripe_onetime_purchase.png)

## Step 3: Request Credits

If you are unable to purchase credits directly, you can request them from an administrator.

1.  On the **"Buy Credits"** tab, click the **"Request Credits"** button.

    ![Request credits button](/img/site/2.1.3-user-credits-buy_credits-request_credits.png)

2.  A modal will appear, allowing you to enter the number of credits you are requesting and a reason for the request.

3.  Click **"Submit Request"**. The administrator will be notified of your request.

## Step 4: Monitor Your Spending

The RAD Platform provides detailed logs of your credit usage.

### Credit Transactions

1.  Click on the **"Credit Transactions"** tab.
2.  This tab shows a complete history of all your credit transactions, including credits added from purchases or requests, and credits deducted for deployments.

    ![Credit Transactions tab with a list of transactions](/img/site/2.2-user-credits-credit_transactions.png)

3.  You can search for specific transactions or filter the list by date. You can also export the history as a CSV file.

    ![Searching for a transaction](/img/site/2.2.1-user-credits-credit_transactions-search.png)

### Project Costs

1.  Click on the **"Project Costs"** tab.
2.  This tab shows the ongoing costs associated with your active deployments. This helps you understand which projects are consuming the most credits.

    ![Project Costs tab showing a breakdown of costs by project](/img/site/2.3-user-credits-project_costs.png)

## Verification

You have successfully learned how to manage your credits. You can now confidently manage your spending on the RAD Platform.
