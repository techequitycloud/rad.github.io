## 1. Introduction
The Finance role provides deep visibility into the platform's economics.

## 2. Step 1: Access Billing Dashboard
1.  Click **Billing** in the navigation bar.
2.  You will see several tabs including **Subscription Tiers**, **Credit Settings**, **Project Costs**, **User Revenue**, **Partner Revenue**, etc.

## 3. Step 2: Analyze Revenue
1.  Click the **Partner Revenue** tab (labeled **Project Revenue** in some views).
    *   **True Revenue:** This metric excludes free credits. It shows actual money earned from credit purchases consumed by deployments.
    *   **Filter:** Filter by date or specific project IDs.
2.  Click **User Revenue**.
    *   See which users are your top spenders.
    *   Drill down into specific user activities.

## 4. Step 3: Credit Management
1.  Click **Credit Settings** to adjust global rates (Price Per Credit, Sign-up Bonus).
2.  Click **Credit Management** (User Management) to adjust individual user balances.

## 5. Step 4: Invoices
1.  Click **Project Invoices**.
2.  Generate and view monthly invoices for compliance and reporting.
