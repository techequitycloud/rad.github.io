# Partner Revenue (Admin View)

The **Partner Revenue** tab allows Finance users and Partners to view revenue generated by modules. This view focuses on the performance of specific modules and the revenue they generate for the platform and partners.

## Features

*   **Filter by Partner:** Select specific partners (module authors) to view their revenue.
    *   If you are a **Partner**, your email is automatically selected.
    *   If you are a **Finance User** or **Admin**, you can select one or multiple partners.
*   **Filter by Module:** Optionally filter by specific module names to see granular performance data.
*   **Date Range Filtering:** Specify a start and end date for the report.
*   **Revenue Summary:** View total transactions, total credit cost, and total revenue for the selected period.
*   **Detailed Table:**
    *   **Date:** The date of the transaction.
    *   **Module Name:** The name of the deployed module.
    *   **User Email:** The user who deployed the module.
    *   **Credit Cost:** The cost in credits for that transaction.
    *   **Revenue:** The revenue amount.
*   **Export to CSV:** Download the data for external analysis.

## How to Use

1.  Navigate to the **Billing** page and click on the **Partner Revenue** tab.
2.  **Select Partners:** Use the dropdown to select partners.
3.  **Select Module (Optional):** Filter by a specific module name if desired.
4.  **Select Date Range:** Enter Start and End dates.
5.  **Fetch Data:** Click **Fetch Partner Revenue**.
6.  **Analyze:** Review the table and summary cards.
7.  **Export:** Click **Export to CSV** to save the report.

## Use Cases

*   **Partners:** Track how much revenue your published modules are generating.
*   **Finance:** Audit payouts due to partners based on their module usage.
*   **Analysis:** Identify top-performing modules and partners.
