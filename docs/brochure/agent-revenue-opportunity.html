Commission Mechanics — How True Revenue Protects Your Earnings

True Revenue means your commission is calculated on purchased credits only — not free credits awarded at sign-up or promotional bonuses. This design ensures that your earnings reflect genuine user activity and commercial spend, not platform incentives.

Commissions accumulate monthly against every referred user who purchases credits and deploys on RAD. There is no cap on the number of users you can refer. Your dashboard updates in real time as referred users deploy — no end-of-month reconciliation, no delays, no black box.

Every referred user remains attributed to you for the lifetime of their subscription. As your referral base grows month on month, your passive income grows with it — without displacing previous earnings.
