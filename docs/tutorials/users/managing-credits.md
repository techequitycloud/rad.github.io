# Managing Your Credits and Subscriptions

This tutorial explains how to manage your billing and credits on the RAD Platform. You will learn how to check your credit balance, subscribe to a plan, purchase additional credits, and track your spending.

## What You'll Learn

- How to navigate the **Billing** page.
- How to subscribe to a credit plan.
- How to make one-time credit purchases.
- How to monitor your credit transactions and project costs.

### Prerequisites

- A RAD Platform account.
- You have completed the [Your First Deployment](./first-deployment.md) tutorial.

### Estimated Time

- **10 minutes**

---

## Step 1: Navigate to the Billing Page

All billing-related activities are managed from the **Billing** page.

1.  From the main navigation menu on the left, click on **"Billing"**.

    ![Main navigation menu with "Billing" highlighted](/img/tutorials/users/nav-menu-billing.png)

2.  The **Billing** page has several tabs: **Subscription Tiers**, **Buy Credits**, **Credit Transactions**, **Project Costs**, and **Monthly Invoices**.

    [SCREENSHOT: Billing page with all the tabs visible]

## Step 2: Subscribe to a Credit Plan

Subscriptions provide you with a recurring monthly credit allowance.

1.  Click on the **"Subscription Tiers"** tab.
2.  You will see a list of available subscription plans, each with a different monthly credit amount and price.

    [SCREENSHOT: Subscription Tiers tab showing available plans]

3.  Choose the plan that best suits your needs and click the **"Subscribe"** button.
4.  You will be redirected to a secure payment page powered by Stripe. Enter your payment details and confirm the subscription.

    [SCREENSHOT: Stripe payment page for subscription]

5.  Once your subscription is active, the plan will be highlighted on the **Subscription Tiers** page.

## Step 3: Purchase Additional Credits

If you need more credits than your subscription provides, you can make a one-time purchase.

1.  Click on the **"Buy Credits"** tab.
2.  Enter the number of credits you wish to purchase.
3.  Click the **"Buy Credits"** button and you will be redirected to Stripe to complete the purchase.

    [SCREENSHOT: "Buy Credits" tab with input for credit amount]

## Step 4: Monitor Your Spending

The RAD Platform provides detailed logs of your credit usage.

### Credit Transactions

1.  Click on the **"Credit Transactions"** tab.
2.  This tab shows a complete history of all your credit transactions, including credits added from subscriptions or purchases, and credits deducted for deployments.

    [SCREENSHOT: Credit Transactions tab with a list of transactions]

3.  You can search for specific transactions or filter the list by date. You can also export the history as a CSV file.

### Project Costs

1.  Click on the **"Project Costs"** tab.
2.  This tab shows the ongoing costs associated with your active deployments. This helps you understand which projects are consuming the most credits.

    [SCREENSHOT: Project Costs tab showing a breakdown of costs by project]

## Verification

You have successfully learned how to manage your credits and subscriptions. You can now confidently manage your spending on the RAD Platform.
